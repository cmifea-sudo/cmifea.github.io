Information Collection and Use ### A) Local Storage (Room Database) The Application functions primarily as an offline log for parenting activities and child growth tracking. All personal data listed below is stored locally, directly on your Android device in a secure sandbox application database (Room Database): * **Personal Names:** Names or nicknames of parents and children. * **Activity Logs:** Timestamps and notes related to children's daily routines (e.g., bedtime, meals, medication/inhalation logs, preschool arrival/leave, play). * **Growth Data:** Physical measurements of children, including weight (grams) and height (centimeters). * **Application Preferences:** Settings regarding language, theme preferences, and color codes. **The Developer does NOT own, operate, or maintain any central servers. Your local application data never leaves your device automatically.** ### B) User-Initiated Backups and Transfers (Google Drive & Local Storage) The Application includes features allowing you to export, back up, or restore your data. These transfers are strictly voluntary and must be initiated by you: * **Google Drive Integration:** If you choose to enable the Google Drive synchronization feature, the Application utilizes the official Google Drive API to upload a structured backup (`.json` file) to your **own private Google Drive storage**. The Developer has no access to your Google Account credentials or the backup files stored in your personal cloud. * **Local File Export (CSV/JSON):** You may export reports or complete database backups to your device's local document storage using the Android Storage Access Framework (SAF). ## 3. Third-Party Services and SDKs To provide specific functionalities, the Application integrates the following trusted third-party services: * **Google Auth / Drive Client Libraries:** Used exclusively to authenticate your Google Account and facilitate secure file transmission between the Application and your personal Google Drive storage. * **Firebase Crashlytics:** Used to capture anonymous crash logs and stack traces to ensure the Application's stability. No personally identifiable information (PII), children's health logs, or activity notes are transmitted to Firebase. ## 4. Permissions Used by the Application The Application requires certain system permissions to function correctly: * `INTERNET`: Required solely to communicate with the Google Drive API for the backup/sync feature and for transmission of anonymous Firebase crash log data. * `POST_NOTIFICATIONS`, `SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM`, `USE_EXACT_ALARM`: Used to trigger local parenting alerts and reminders on your device. * `RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED`: Used to reschedule your local notifications after your device reboots. ## 5. Children's Privacy (COPPA & GDPR Compliance) Although the Application is designed for parents to log information about their children, **the Application does not knowingly collect, request, or harvest personal data from children directly.** All data is input manually by the parent or legal guardian and stays under their exclusive control on their local device or personal cloud. ## 6. Data Deletion and Retention You retain complete ownership and sovereignty over your data: * **In-App Deletion:** You can delete individual log entries, children's profiles, or metadata directly within the Application at any time. * **Complete Purge:** Uninstalling the Application from your device instantly and permanently deletes the local Room database and all preferences stored on the device. * **Cloud Deletion:** You can delete your backup files (`.json` and `.csv`) directly from your personal Google Drive storage at any time. ## 7.
